### Section Features (Fonctionnalités)
Titre : "Powerful Features - Everything you need to scale your impact"
Sous-titre : "Replace fragmented tools with one unified platform designed for the modern nonprofit era."

6 cartes de fonctionnalités :
1. **AI Copilot** - Icône robot/IA
   - Leverage generative AI for nonprofits to get intelligent recommendations on when to ask, how much to ask, and who to target.

2. **Donor Management** - Icône personnes
   - A 360-degree view of your supporters. Track history, preferences, and engagement in one secure nonprofit CRM.

3. **Automated Campaigns** - Icône éclair
   - Set up multi-channel communication flows that nurture donors automatically while you sleep.

4. **Smart Donation Forms** - Icône coeur
   - Conversion-optimized forms that adapt suggested amounts based on donor history and profile.

5. **Impact Analytics** - Icône graphique
   - Real-time dashboards that turn complex data into actionable insights for your board and team.

6. **Secure & Compliant** - Icône bouclier
   - Enterprise-grade security with automated tax receipting and compliance built-in.
